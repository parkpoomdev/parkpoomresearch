## Overview

Research and development of data analytics and visualization for telehealth monitoring and assistive systems at the Asian Institute of Technology (AIT). Work focuses on supporting healthcare professionals with actionable insights for elderly and disabled people.

## Key Contributions

- **Telehealth Analytics**: Designed dashboards and visualizations for fall detection and rehabilitation monitoring.
- **Data Pipeline**: Implemented data collection and preprocessing workflows for sensor and app telemetry.
- **AI + Decision Support**: Applied machine learning to assist healthcare professionals’ decision-making.
- **User-Centered Design**: Collaborated with clinicians to refine insights and usability.

## Technologies Used

- **Python, NodeJS, SQL**: Data processing and backend services.
- **React/Next.js**: Frontend dashboards and visualization interfaces.
- **Visualization**: Charting and custom visual components for clinical metrics.

## Challenges and Learnings

Key challenges included aligning model outputs with clinical workflows and ensuring data quality from heterogeneous sources. Iterative co-design with healthcare professionals improved interpretability and real-world utility.

## Outcome

Delivered prototypes and studies demonstrating improved monitoring and clearer insights for rehabilitation and fall detection scenarios. The work supports ongoing research at AIT and contributes to publications.
